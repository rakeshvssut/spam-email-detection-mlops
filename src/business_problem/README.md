# Business Problem

## Goal
Classify incoming email text into:
- `spam` (unwanted or fraudulent)
- `ham` (legitimate)

## Why this stage matters
A clear business definition avoids building a technically good model that does not solve the real problem.

## What to define here
- Decision threshold objective (higher precision vs higher recall for spam)
- Success metrics (F1, precision, recall)
- Business constraints (latency, acceptable false positives)

## Inputs
- Stakeholder requirements
- Product risk tolerance

## Outputs
- Problem statement
- Metric targets
- Acceptance criteria
